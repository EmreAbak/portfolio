Demand Forecast Analysis

Overview

Demand forecast analysis is a strategic analysis method that aims to predict future product or service demand based on past data and current trends. These analyses play a fundamental role in optimizing inventory management, production planning, and customer satisfaction. Through data-based predictions, resources are used more efficiently and processes are optimized.

Purpose of Application

  • Optimizing production, inventory, and supply planning by correctly predicting future demand
  • Preventing problems such as excess or insufficient inventory
  • Increasing customer satisfaction and evaluating sales opportunities in a timely manner
  • Making decision-making processes data-driven

Application Process

  • Data Collection: Past sales data, seasonal fluctuations, campaign effects, and external factors are analyzed.
  • Modeling: Time series analysis, regression, moving averages, or AI-based prediction models are used.
  • Segmentation: Separate analyses are performed according to product groups, regional demand, and customer profiles.
  • Scenario Analysis: Alternative predictions are created for different situations (seasonal, sudden increase/decrease, promotion).
  • Reporting: Results are visualized and presented to relevant departments to integrate into the planning process.

Results and Contributions

  • Increase in accuracy in demand forecasts
  • Optimized inventory management
  • Increased supply chain efficiency
  • Increased customer satisfaction
  • Reduced operational costs
