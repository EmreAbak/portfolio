Work Analysis Studies

Overview

Work analysis studies aim to define all processes and positions in companies clearly, determine the workload correctly, and measure the efficiency of the workforce. These studies allow organizations to use their workforce more efficiently, plan the workforce correctly, and improve processes. Thanks to work analysis, workload is calculated for each employee, and norm kadro analyses are created without the distinction between white and blue collar workers.

During the analysis process, the job descriptions of the employees are clearly defined, and the workload in each position is analyzed in detail. The obtained data helps strategic decisions to improve the operational efficiency of the company. Thanks to workload analyses, unnecessary workforce losses and balanced workload distribution are prevented.

Purpose of Application

  • The correct determination of the workload
  • Increasing the efficiency of the workforce
  • The implementation of norm kadro analyses
  • Preventing imbalance in workforce in processes
  • The clear definition of job descriptions of employees

Application Process

  • Detailed observations are made on the processes, and the jobs of the employees are determined
  • The workload in each position is measured, and time analysis is performed
  • Workload calculations are made separately for white and blue collar workers
  • The workload data is combined and norm kadro analyses are performed and reported
  • The obtained data is shared with management and recommendations are made for workforce optimization

Results and Contributions

  • The efficiency of the workforce is increased
  • The imbalance in workload between employees is eliminated
  • Unnecessary workforce and costs are eliminated with norm kadro calculations
  • Workforce planning becomes more accurate and efficient
  • The processes become more efficient and sustainable
