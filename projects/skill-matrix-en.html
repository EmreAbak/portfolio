Employee Special Skill Matrix Forms

General Overview

Employee Special Skill Matrix Forms are tools used to track and evaluate the skills, abilities, and development areas of employees in companies. Through these forms, the strong points and development areas of each employee are identified, education needs are identified, and career development plans are created.

Application Purpose

  • The systematic evaluation of the skills and abilities of employees
  • Identification of education and development needs
  • Optimization of job rotation and task distribution
  • Creation of career development plans
  • Creation of organizational skill maps

Application Process

  • Determination of required skills and abilities for positions
  • Creation of evaluation criteria and measurement systems
  • Evaluation of employees' current skills
  • Identification of development areas and preparation of education plans
  • Regular tracking and updating processes

Results and Contributions

  • Employee development is tracked systematically
  • Education programs are planned more effectively
  • Workforce efficiency increases
  • Career development processes become more transparent
  • Organizational skill level increases
