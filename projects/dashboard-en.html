Dashboard Studies

General Overview

Dashboard studies, which aim to visualize data analysis in a visual way, accelerate and facilitate decision-making processes based on data. These panels help monitor business processes, evaluate performance indicators, and increase operational efficiency.

Dashboards transform raw data into meaningful visual components through user-friendly interfaces, allowing managers and teams to quickly access operational status information and make informed decisions. These studies are typically used to save time, analyze data faster, and improve process transparency.

Application Purpose

  • Making data more understandable through visual tools
  • Creating a centralized system where performance indicators are aggregated
  • Providing quick information access and immediate feedback
  • Making relevant processes traceable and reportable

Application Process

  • Need Analysis: At the first stage, it is determined which indicators will be tracked and which data will be used
  • Collection of Data Sources: Different data sources (excel, database, ERP systems, etc.) are collected and organized
  • Dashboard Design: Appropriate visualizations and graphs are created according to the need
  • Data Integration: Data from different sources is integrated and displayed on a single panel
  • Testing and Updating: Panels are tested and validated, and improvement processes are started if necessary

Results and Contributions

  • Decision-making processes are accelerated by providing quick access to data
  • Reporting processes are simplified and manual error rates are reduced
  • Performance indicators are clearly tracked and the success rates of targets are evaluated
  • Collaboration between managers and teams is increased through the visualization of operational processes
