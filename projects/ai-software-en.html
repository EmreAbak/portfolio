Artificial Intelligence and Software Studies

General Overview

Artificial intelligence and software studies are development activities carried out to simplify, automate, and improve processes using digital tools. These studies provide effective solutions to reduce operational load, support data-driven decision-making processes, and accelerate internal information flow. By effectively using ready-made systems and developing process-specific solutions, manual workload is reduced, error rates are lowered, and processes become more sustainable.

Application Purpose

  • To make processes faster, more regular, and measurable
  • To automate repetitive tasks and save time and resources
  • To support decision-making processes by presenting data in a simple and meaningful way
  • To make processes traceable, reportable, and standardized

Application Process

  • A solution model is developed by analyzing the needs of the process.
  • Data sources are determined and converted into an analyzable structure.
  • Digital panels are created using ready-made platforms (Excel, Power BI, Google Sheets, etc.).
  • Automated calculations, basic forecasting structures, and visual reporting tools are developed.
  • The outputs are shared regularly with the relevant units and used in decision-making processes.

Results and Contributions

  • Automation and productivity increase in processes
  • Decision-making processes become AI-supported
  • Repetitive tasks are automated and error rates decrease
  • Data processing and analysis processes speed up
  • Digital transformation goals are achieved
