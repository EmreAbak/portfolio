Data Analysis Studies

General Overview

Data analysis studies are conducted to improve the understanding of business processes, monitor performance, and support decision-making processes with numerical data. Through these studies, past data is examined, trends are identified, and insights related to processes are obtained. In the analysis process, data from different sources is collected, organized, and structured to make them meaningful and understandable.

Application Purpose

  • Supporting a data-driven thinking culture in decision-making processes
  • Measuring and identifying improvement areas in processes
  • Simplifying complex data to make them easily understandable
  • Shortening reporting times and standardizing analysis processes

Application Process

  • Data from various sources is collected for relevant processes
  • Missing, incorrect, or repeated data is cleaned and prepared for analysis
  • Basic statistical analyses are performed according to the determined indicators
  • Time series, comparisons, and performance measurements are made
  • The results are reported in visual tables and graphs

Results and Contributions

  • Decision-making processes are supported by data
  • Performance measurements become regular and transparent
  • Reporting processes are standardized and accelerated
  • Improvement areas are clearly visible through data
