Time Study

General Overview

Time study is a process of evaluating, measuring, and analyzing each step of an operation. This work is crucial for improving operational efficiency, optimizing resource utilization, and making operations more efficient. Time study is used as a tool to improve workforce management and achieve organizational goals.

Application Purpose

  • Identifying time losses in operations to improve efficiency
  • Ensuring that employees perform their tasks more efficiently
  • Developing strategies to make processes faster and more efficient
  • Optimizing resource utilization and reducing costs

Application Process

  • First, each operation is observed and measured in detail.
  • Each step in the process is evaluated in terms of time, based on the current work flow.
  • The data obtained shows how long it takes to complete the tasks and which areas can be improved.
  • After the necessary analyses are performed, the areas to be improved in the processes are determined and adjustments are made accordingly.
  • All activities are supported by time study reports and the results are presented to the relevant units.

Results and Contributions

  • Operational efficiency is increased in production processes
  • Workforce losses are identified and prevented
  • Standard operation times are determined
  • Capacity calculations are more accurate
  • Cost and planning processes are improved
