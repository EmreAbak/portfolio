Visual Management

General Overview

Visual management activities are a management system that allows the monitoring and management of processes, performance, and goals with visual tools. This system aims to share information quickly and effectively, detect problems early, and accelerate problem-solving processes. Visual management tools encourage active participation in processes and support continuous improvement culture.

Application Purpose

  • Monitoring and management of processes and performance with visual tools
  • Acceleration of information flow and transparency
  • Early detection of problems and rapid problem-solving
  • Increased employee participation and awareness
  • Supporting continuous improvement culture

Application Process

  • Determining visual management needs and goals
  • Designing and implementing suitable visual management tools
  • Creating performance indicators and tracking systems
  • Providing employee training and ensuring effective use of the system
  • Regular review and update processes

Results and Contributions

  • Increased process efficiency and performance
  • Enhanced communication and information sharing
  • Problems are detected and solved faster
  • Increased employee participation and motivation
  • Continuous improvement culture is established

Note: Details of other visual management activities are not shared due to confidentiality.
